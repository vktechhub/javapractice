MongoDB:- 

CURD operations:- 
1. Create:- db.collection.insertOne(), db.collection.insertMany()
2. Read:- db.collection.find(), db.collection.findOne()
3. Update:- db.collection.updateOne(), db.collection.updateMany(), db.collection.replaceOne()
4. Delete:- db.collection.deleteOne(), db.collection.deleteMany()

Indexing:-
1. Single Field Index:- db.collection.createIndex({field: 1 or -1})
2. Compound Index:- db.collection.createIndex({field1: 1 or -1, field2: 1 or -1})
3. Text Index:- db.collection.createIndex({field: "text"})
4. Unique Index:- db.collection.createIndex({field: 1}, {unique: true})
5. TTL Index:- db.collection.createIndex({field: 1}, {expireAfterSeconds: seconds})

Aggregation Framework:-
1. $match:- Filters documents
2. $group:- Groups documents by a specified identifier
3. $project:- Reshapes documents
4. $sort:- Sorts documents
5. $limit:- Limits the number of documents
6. $lookup:- Performs left outer join with another collection
7. $unwind:- Deconstructs an array field
8. $addFields:- Adds new fields to documents
9. $count:- Counts the number of documents
10. $facet:- Processes multiple aggregation pipelines within a single stage
